THE BENCH

Practitioners.

Senior trainers, Agile coaches, Scrum Masters and Product Owners, accredited and on panel. The team that turns up to do the work, named in aggregate, not in headcount inflation.

Who they are

Four disciplines. One bench.

Senior trainers

Lead cohort delivery, accredited under the CPA framework. Run open and closed cohorts up to 200 participants.

Agile coaches

Embed in transformation programmes, working alongside leadership and delivery teams to install and sustain new ways of working.

Scrum Masters

Embedded with delivery teams. Facilitate cadence, surface impediments, and protect the team's commitments at the source.

Product Owners

Embedded with delivery teams or seconded to the business. Hold the value decision, in dialogue with strategy and stakeholders.
