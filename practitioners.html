Our people

Practitioners, not presenters.

Everyone on the Agile Capability team has delivered transformations in the field. We do not send slide-writers. We send people who have done it - in complex organisations, in multiple sectors, across multiple geographies.

The bench

Four disciplines. One team.

Every engagement draws on a combination of these four practice areas. The right mix depends on where your organisation is and what it needs.

01

Senior Trainers

Certified instructors who design and deliver the programmes that build agile capability at scale. Deep expertise in adult learning, certification pathways, and curriculum design. They train the practitioners who run your transformation.

02

Agile Coaches

Senior practitioners who work at the organisational level - helping leadership teams understand what they need to change, facilitating the ACT diagnostic, and holding the strategic thread of a transformation engagement. The people your leadership team works with directly.

03

Scrum Masters

Embedded practitioners who work inside your delivery teams. They run the ceremonies, surface the impediments, coach the team on self-organisation, and build the muscle memory that outlasts the engagement. The people doing the daily work.

04

Product Owners

Specialists in product thinking and backlog management who work alongside your own product people to build genuine ownership capability. They understand the difference between proxy ownership and real product authority - and how to make the transition.
