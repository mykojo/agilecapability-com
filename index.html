Agile Capability Test

Start with an honest picture.

The ACT diagnostic maps your organisation across twelve dimensions. It tells you where you are strong, where you are fragile, and what to fix first. No sales pitch embedded. Just an accurate read.

01
Leadership Alignment
Do senior leaders share a coherent picture of what the transformation is for and how it will work?
02
Team Structure
Are teams sized, scoped, and staffed to own and deliver value end to end?
03
Product Thinking
Is there genuine product ownership - empowered to set direction and make trade-offs - or just proxy ownership?
04
Technical Practices
Are engineering practices keeping pace with delivery ambition? CI/CD, testing, deployment pipelines.
05
Feedback Loops
How quickly does real information about outcomes reach the people who can act on it?
06
Governance & Funding
Does how you plan and fund work support or contradict how you say you want to work?
07
Culture & Safety
Can teams surface problems early without risk to themselves? Is failure used as data or as blame?
08
Practitioner Capability
Do the people running agile processes actually understand them - and can they coach others?
