Service

Senior Advisory

Direct engagement with C-suite and senior leadership. We help your leaders understand what agility actually means for your organisation - and what to do first.

Leadership is the leverage point.

Most transformations stall because leadership teams carry conflicting mental models of what the transformation is for. Agile is not a delivery methodology to be bolted onto an unchanged organisation. It is a fundamentally different approach to how decisions are made, how funding flows, and how accountability works.

Our Senior Advisory service creates the leadership alignment that transformations require but rarely get. We work directly with your most senior team - board level where relevant, always executive level - to build a shared and accurate picture of what you are signing up for.

This is not a workshop on agile principles. It is a serious working engagement with practitioners who have navigated the same challenges in comparable organisations.

Who this is for

Senior leadership teams in organisations beginning, accelerating, or rescuing a transformation. CTO, CPO, COO, and their direct reports. Boards who need to understand what they are funding.

Typical engagement

2-4 months. Structured working sessions with the leadership team, typically monthly. Supported by the ACT assessment to establish a baseline and track alignment over time.

What we cover

The questions that matter.

1

Why are we transforming?

Establishing the real commercial driver - not the version that sounds good in a press release. Speed to market, cost base, talent retention, competitive positioning. Different drivers require different approaches.

2

What does done look like?

Defining measurable success criteria that your leadership team will stand behind 18 months from now. Not "we are more agile" - specific, observable, commercially meaningful outcomes.

3

What are we willing to change?

Every transformation requires some leadership team to change how they work. Governance, funding cycles, how performance is assessed, how escalations flow. Understanding your appetite for this shapes the programme.

4

What will we protect?

There are things in your organisation that work. The transformation should not break them. Identifying what to protect is as important as identifying what to change.

Start with a conversation.

Tell us where you are in your transformation and what is not working. We will tell you honestly whether and how we can help.
