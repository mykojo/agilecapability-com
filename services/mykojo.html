Platform capabilities

Everything the ACT assessment needs. And more.

Survey administration

Distribute the ACT survey across your respondent population. Manage invitations, track completion rates, and chase non-completers. All from one dashboard.

Real-time aggregation

See results build as respondents complete the survey. Aggregated across teams, business units, leadership levels, and geographies in real time.

Diagnostic reports

Automated report generation covering all 12 ACT dimensions, with scoring, interpretation, and prioritised recommendations. Ready to present to your leadership team.

Benchmark comparison

Compare your scores against sector and company-size benchmarks. Understand where you are ahead of the curve and where you are trailing.

Cycle tracking

Run the ACT quarterly and track improvement dimension by dimension. See whether your interventions are moving the dial - with evidence, not anecdote.

Practitioner dashboard

For Agile Capability coaches running engagements across multiple clients. Manage assessments, track progress, and prepare readout materials in one place.

How it works

Three steps to your diagnostic.

1

Set up your assessment

Define your respondent groups - leadership, product, teams. Add participants by email. Set a completion deadline. MyKojo handles the invitations and reminders.

2

Collect responses

Respondents complete the survey online - 25-35 minutes, device agnostic. You see completion rates in real time. The platform flags when you have reached statistical significance per dimension.

3

Review your results

Your diagnostic report is generated automatically once all responses are in. Your Agile Capability practitioner facilitates a 2-hour leadership readout to walk through the findings.
