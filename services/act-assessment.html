The ACT assessment.

A two-to-three-week diagnostic. We map the working reality of your organisation against the capabilities required for the strategy you have committed to. Where you are strong. Where you are exposed. What is moving, what is stuck, and what the next ninety days should look like.

Who it is for

Start here. Whatever comes next.

Anyone considering one of our other services

Most engagements start here. The assessment defines what the work actually needs to be.

CEOs and Boards setting strategy

When the strategy is clear and the question is whether the organisation actually has the capability to deliver it.

COOs running a programme that is off-plan

When the dashboards are green but the outcomes are not, and you need a second, evidence-led read.

What you get

Four things, reliably.

01

A capability map across up to 25 teams, scored against the strategy you have committed to.

02

A small number of unavoidable truths, ranked by economic impact.

03

A 90-day recommendation. What to start, what to stop, and what to leave alone.

04

An evidence-led conversation with your leadership team, not a hundred-slide deck.
