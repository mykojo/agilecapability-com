Service

Capability Building

Training and certification programmes for agile practitioners, scrum masters, product owners, and the leaders who sponsor them. Practical skills that transfer to real work on day one.

Skills over certificates.

Certification is the outcome of developing real skill - not the substitute for it. Our Capability Building programmes are designed by practitioners who have used these skills to deliver real transformations, and they are tested against real scenarios, not exam banks.

We deliver through the Professional Agile framework, which means your people come out with recognised certifications. But the thing we are most interested in is whether they can actually do the work on Monday morning.

Every cohort is capped to keep the quality of the learning environment high. We do not run the same programme for 200 people in a conference hall.

Delivery formats

In-person intensive (2-3 days), virtual cohort over 4 weeks, or blended. Programme selected based on your team composition and geography. We have delivered programmes across Africa, Americas, and Europe.

Certification

Programmes lead to Professional Agile certifications including CPA (Certified Professional Agilist) and CPAF (CPA Foundations). Recognised internationally. No expiry date - continuous professional practice counts.

Programmes

What we offer.

Foundation

CPA Foundations

Entry-level certification for practitioners new to agile ways of working. Covers the core principles, team dynamics, backlog management, sprint ceremonies, and what good looks like. Leads to CPAF certification.

Practitioner

Certified Professional Agilist

For experienced practitioners who are coaching teams or leading agile adoption in their organisation. Deeper content on scaling, stakeholder management, and measurement. Leads to CPA certification.

Leadership

Agile for Leaders

A non-certification programme for senior leaders who need to understand agile deeply enough to sponsor it effectively - without needing to become practitioners themselves. Highly practical, case-based format.
